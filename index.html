About Me

I am Oswaldo Cortes, a proud Latino who is currently a sophomore pursuing a degree in computer science. Two years ago, I decided to join the CSin3 program, a rigorous program for students who seek to obtain a bachelor's degree in computer science in three years. The workload has pushed me to the limit countless times, but with perseverance and determination, I have been able to succeed.

Throughout my academic journey, I have never forgotten the reason why I chose computer science. The fact that computer science is nearly endless is enough to satisfy my thirst for knowledge. Despite the workload, I managed to find a way to give back to the program by mentoring incoming Freshmen and guiding students on hands-on raspberry pi pico projects that inspired them.

My mentees had their first hands-on experience with software and hardware, and I enjoyed passing on my knowledge of the project to them. For this reason, I am interested in the Google STEP internship. I want to help Google with its mission of providing accessible and reliable information to the rest of the world. I'm looking forward to meeting new people and working on developing solutions to challenging problems that benefit society as a whole. Thank you!

CSin3 Program Member

The CSin3 Program is an educational program in which Hartnell College and CSUMB have partnered up to provide students with a 3 year pathway in which they will work towards their Bachelors degree in computer science. Students complete half of their course work at Hartnell College and the other half at CSUMB.
